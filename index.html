Housing loan - Case study

This case study deals with the common scenario of housing loans where customers approach a bank for a housing loan. The application is scrutinized by the bank and then grants them loan. Customers repay loan in installments.

Assumptions

  • Bank is capable to grant as many loans as possible
  • Bank does not reject any loan. It instead modifies the loan terms and amount if needed
  • Payment failed condition not handled in repayment scenario to avoid complications
  • Borrower already has an existing account in the concerned bank
  • Borrower always repays the loan, may or may not be on time
